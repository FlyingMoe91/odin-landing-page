Let's talk about rays!

There are many different kind of rays. Here you can get a quick overview of the different types

Here are four different types of rays

Manta Rays

Manta rays are are the largest rays in the world. They feel home in tropical, subtropical and temperate ocean waters.

Eagle Rays

There are three different kinds of eagle rays. They can e found in tropical and warm-temperate waters around the globe

Sting Rays

Sting rays are found in the shallow coastal waters of temperate seas. There are about 220 known species.

Skate Rays

Skates are found in most parts of the world. As the water becomes more shallow and warmer, skates are seen to be replaced by stingrays.
